Hello, I'm Isha, an Information and Experience Designer, recently crafting GenAI led enterprise experiences at TCS.

To me, UX design is about humanizing technology. I strive to create seamlessly intuitive experiences to make life simpler for everybody. I’ve always considered people to be at the heart of the design process and I believe that creative collaboration between design and technology is the magic that takes experiences from good to great!

I've freshly graduated with B.Des in User Experience Design from MIT Institute of Design, Pune, and specialize in UX Research, UI Design and Design Thinking.

I think when we understand human-human interaction, only then can we design efficient human-technology interaction. I love having conversations with different people; I think that helps me communicate with my team efficiently and bring a surge of energy to every project I work on.

I would love to know your ideas. Send me a mail and we can collaborate to create something amazing!
